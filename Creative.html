    Make your own micro wind turbine electricity:


    Collect some blade.


    Make a turbine as like fan that can rotate with the power of wind flow.


    Now make a pillar and stant it in the place where you want to install windfirm.


    Now attach the turbine in the top of the pillar and install it tightly


    Now attach a generator behind the turbine shaft.


    Now let the turbine rotate with the flow of wind.


    Insert an output wire on the generator.


    Done.


    Wow ! Amazing ! You have made micro wind turbine electricity :)


    Make your own Micro hydro turbine electricity:

    Make a fan/turbine as like a wheel attaching blade around it.(For more take a look on google image)


    Find a small river or drain that flows water.


    Now make a suitable position to setup this turbine on the drain/river.


    Now immerse one-half of this turbine on the river water.


    Now attach a generator on the turbine shaft.


    Let the turbine rotate.


    Wire the generator for output power.


    Done.


    Wow ! Amazing ! You have made micro hydro turbine electricity :)
